Frequently Asked Questions

What are BankWow's operating hours? +

Our branches are typically open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Online and mobile banking are available 24/7.

How do I report a lost or stolen card? +

Immediately call our 24/7 support line at 1-800-BANKWOW or log into your online banking account to report it. You can also freeze your card instantly via the mobile app.

Can I open an account online? +

Yes, many of our checking and savings accounts can be opened conveniently online in just a few minutes. Visit our Accounts page for more details.
